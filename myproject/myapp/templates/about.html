Our Story

From Passion to Profession

Founded in 2015 by a team of passionate car enthusiasts, AutoShine began as a small garage operation with a simple mission: to provide car owners with professional-grade products that deliver exceptional results.

What started as a weekend hobby quickly grew into a full-scale business when we realized the lack of quality, affordable car care products in the market. We spent years researching, testing, and perfecting our formulations to create products that not only clean and protect but also enhance the natural beauty of every vehicle.

Today, AutoShine serves thousands of customers worldwide, from weekend warriors to professional detailers, all united by their love for maintaining pristine vehicles.
